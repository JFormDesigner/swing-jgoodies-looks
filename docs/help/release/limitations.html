Limitations

Paint Glitches When Switching from Non-JGoodies Looks

The JGoodies Looks can properly change of the look and theme at runtime.

Several other Look&Feel implementations prevent a proper change of the look and may cause paint problems after you've choosen such a look in the Launcher.

Can't Simulate Resolution on 1.4 or Later

The screen resolution simulation modifies the JGoodies Looks good guess algorithm that is used in Java 1.3 environments, only. Hence, this feature is disabled 1.4 environments.
